Admission Process

At Nyapui Secondary School of Excellence, we welcome students who are eager to learn, grow, and contribute to our vibrant community. Our admission process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our challenging academic environment and contribute positively to our school culture.

Senior Secondary School Admission Requirement

Admission to our senior school begins promptly after the release of the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) results. The process requires:

Step 1

Photocopy of BECE result/testimonial

Step 2

Two passport photos

Step 3

Copy of birth certificate

Final Step

A student must be in her previous school uniform




Junior Secondary School Admission Requirement

Similar procedure for senior school is required to students to gain admission into JSS1. It follows shortly upon the release of the National Primary School Examination (NPSE) result. The process requires:

Step 1

A photocopy of NPSE result/testimonial

Step 2

Two passport photos

Step 3

Copy of birth certificate

Final Step

Submit application in school uniform
