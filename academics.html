Coding Club

This Club is composed of specialized sub-clubs that focus on coding, programming, and data analytics. Each of these sub-clubs offers tailored projects, mentorship, and activities that allow students to develop foundational skills in software development, problem-solving, and data-driven decision-making. Our mission is to prepare students to be innovators and leaders in the rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Cultural and Drama Club

The Cultural & Drama Club at Nyapui Secondary School is a dynamic space where students come together to celebrate diversity, explore cultural expressions, and hone their theatrical talents. This club provides a platform for students to engage in various forms of performance art, including drama, dance, music, and storytelling, while also delving into the rich cultural heritage of our community and beyond.
